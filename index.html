How to Identify Your Skin Tone

Vein Test

Look at the veins on your wrist in natural light:

  • Blue/Purple veins: Cool undertones
  • Green veins: Warm undertones
  • Blue-green veins: Neutral undertones

Jewelry Test

Which metal looks better on you?

  • Silver jewelry: Cool undertones
  • Gold jewelry: Warm undertones
  • Both look good: Neutral undertones

Sun Reaction

How does your skin react to sun exposure?

  • Burns easily, tans poorly: Cool undertones
  • Tans easily, rarely burns: Warm undertones
  • Moderate tanning: Neutral undertones
